Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept products for review?

We research products independently. We do not accept free products in exchange for positive reviews. Our editorial process is not influenced by manufacturers.

Can I request a review of a specific product?

Yes. If you'd like us to review a supplement or health product, send us the name and where it's sold. We'll evaluate it against our standard research criteria and publish a review if it meets our relevance threshold.

I found incorrect information in a review. What should I do?

Please contact us with the specific correction and any supporting sources. We take accuracy seriously and will update content promptly when errors are identified.

Do you have an affiliate relationship with the products you review?

Yes, and we disclose this clearly. We participate in affiliate programs and may earn a commission when you purchase through our links. This does not affect our ratings or recommendations. Read our full Affiliate Disclosure.
