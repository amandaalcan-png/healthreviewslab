Health Reviews Labs Verdict

Neuro Serge's formula is focused rather than padded with filler ingredients, every component has a documented mechanism related to cognition, and doses align with what's typically used in published research.

Lion's Mane Mushroom Extract — 500mg

Lion's Mane (Hericium erinaceus) is studied for its potential to stimulate nerve growth factor (NGF) production, a protein essential for neuron growth, maintenance, and survival. A 2009 Phytotherapy Research study followed older adults over 16 weeks and found measurable cognitive improvements compared to placebo, with effects diminishing after supplementation stopped, suggesting an active ongoing mechanism.

Bacopa Monnieri — 300mg

A traditional Ayurvedic herb with substantial modern clinical research behind it. Multiple randomized controlled trials have shown improved memory recall scores after 8-12 weeks of consistent supplementation, with the effect specifically tied to memory consolidation, the process of converting short-term memories into stable long-term storage.

Ginkgo Biloba — 120mg

One of the most extensively studied herbal extracts for circulation. Applied to cognitive function, improved cerebral blood flow supports better oxygen and nutrient delivery to neural tissue, which is particularly relevant as vascular efficiency naturally declines with age.

Phosphatidylserine — 100mg

A phospholipid that forms a structural part of brain cell membranes. Research has suggested that supplementation, particularly in older adults whose natural levels decline with age, may support memory function and overall cognitive performance.

Supporting B-Vitamins

B-vitamins, particularly B6, B9 (folate), and B12, play essential roles in neurotransmitter synthesis and homocysteine metabolism. Elevated homocysteine has been linked in research to increased risk of cognitive decline, making these vitamins a relevant supporting addition to the core nootropic formula.
