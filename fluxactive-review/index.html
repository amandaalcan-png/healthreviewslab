Ingredients — Full Analysis

Saw Palmetto

The most clinically validated botanical prostate ingredient. Inhibits 5-alpha reductase — reducing DHT conversion that directly drives prostate cell proliferation. Multiple meta-analyses confirm meaningful improvements in urinary symptom scores. The foundational ingredient of any serious prostate formula, present here at a therapeutic standardized dose.

Ginkgo Biloba

Improves microcirculation throughout the body including prostate tissue. The terpenoid and flavonoid compounds in Ginkgo support endothelial function and nitric oxide production — maintaining the blood flow that prostate tissue needs to stay healthy. Also provides neuroprotective effects that support bladder nerve function.

Oat Straw

Avena sativa provides adaptogenic support for cortisol regulation — chronically elevated cortisol impairs testosterone production and promotes the inflammatory environment that worsens BPH. Also provides B vitamins, zinc, and silica that support prostate cellular health.

Chinese Ginseng (Panax)

Ginsenoside compounds support testosterone production through LH stimulation, reduce systemic inflammation through NF-κB pathway modulation, and provide antioxidant protection for prostate cells. One of the most studied adaptogens in men's health research with consistent evidence across multiple clinical trials.

Damiana

Turnera diffusa leaf with traditional use for male urinary and reproductive health. Modern research shows flavonoid compounds that reduce smooth muscle tension in the bladder and urethra — directly addressing the urgency and incomplete emptying symptoms of BPH through a muscular relaxation mechanism.

Important: Ginkgo Biloba and Panax Ginseng in this formula interact with blood thinners (warfarin, aspirin, clopidogrel) — consult physician before use if anticoagulated. Men on alpha-blockers or 5-alpha reductase inhibitors should discuss adding this formula with their urologist. Not a replacement for prescribed prostate medications.
