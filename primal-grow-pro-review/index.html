What the Research Actually Supports

Improved blood flow quality directly affects erection firmness, achieved through L-Citrulline's conversion to L-Arginine and then nitric oxide in the body — a more efficient pathway than supplementing arginine directly, since citrulline bypasses the liver metabolism that limits arginine's bioavailability. Testosterone support affects energy, drive, and performance — Tribulus Terrestris has research specifically on supporting luteinizing hormone (LH), the signal that triggers the body's own testosterone production. Natural PDE5 inhibition works through the same mechanism as pharmaceutical ED treatments, through a natural pathway — Icariin from Horny Goat Weed is the relevant compound here. And libido improvement has a strong independent evidence base specifically around Maca Root, one of the most replicated findings in this category. Together, these four ingredients address the vascular, hormonal, and neurological systems involved in male sexual function simultaneously, rather than focusing on just one.
