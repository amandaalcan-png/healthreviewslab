The Distinction Every Nerve Supplement Review Should Make

Peripheral nerve discomfort has two distinct components that respond to entirely different interventions. Structural nerve damage — from diabetic neuropathy, B12 deficiency, or physical injury — requires targeted medical treatment. Nervous system hypersensitivity — the amplification of pain signals that makes mild symptoms far more intense than the underlying damage produces on its own — is where botanical supplementation has legitimate mechanistic relevance. The difference between these two problems is rarely explained clearly in supplement marketing. We explain it for every product we review in this category, because it's the most important factor in determining whether a formula fits your situation.
