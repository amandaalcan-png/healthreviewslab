About Health Reviews Labs

Independent editorial analysis of health supplement formulas — covering ingredients, research, and consumer feedback.

What We Do

Health Reviews Labs is an independent editorial platform dedicated to the analysis of health supplement formulas. We examine active ingredients against published clinical research, assess consumer feedback patterns from verified sources, verify official website claims, and provide honest purchasing guidance — so that when you search for information about a supplement, you find something more useful than a sales page.

We are not doctors. We do not prescribe, diagnose, or treat medical conditions. What we do is research — systematically and editorially — across four core health and wellness categories.

Men's Health & Prostate

Prostate support, testosterone, nitric oxide, and male vitality formulas for men over 40.

Blood Sugar & Metabolic Health

Glucose regulation, insulin sensitivity, weight management, and metabolic rate formulas.

Nail, Skin & Specialty

Nail fungus treatments, gut-skin axis supplements, eye health, hearing, and cognitive support.

Pet Health

Canine probiotic and digestive health supplements analyzed against veterinary research.

Our Editorial Process

Every analysis published on Health Reviews Labs follows the same framework:

  • Ingredient research — each active compound analyzed against publicly available clinical literature, including PubMed-indexed studies where available
  • Dose assessment — whether ingredients are present at therapeutically meaningful doses, not token amounts
  • Manufacturing verification — GMP certification status, FDA-registered facility credentials, and third-party testing disclosures
  • Complaints investigation — real consumer complaints sourced from verified purchase platforms and assessed honestly
  • Claims verification — official website claims checked against what the research actually supports
  • Purchasing safety — authorized seller identification, counterfeit risk assessment, and guarantee term evaluation

What We Don't Do

  • We do not test products personally or claim first-hand user experience
  • We do not accept payment for positive coverage
  • We do not make medical claims or provide treatment recommendations
  • We do not fabricate testimonials or user results
  • We do not recommend products without ingredient-level research backing

Editorial Independence: Our analysis is not influenced by commercial relationships. Some pages on this site contain affiliate links — we may earn a commission if you purchase through our links. This does not change what we write or how we assess products. Affiliate relationships are disclosed on every relevant page and in our Affiliate Disclosure.
