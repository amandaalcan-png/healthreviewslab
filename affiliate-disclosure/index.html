Affiliate Disclosure

Full transparency on how our affiliate relationships work.

Summary: Health Reviews Labs earns commissions on purchases made through links on this site. This does not cost you anything extra, and it does not influence our editorial analysis or recommendations.

What This Means

Health Reviews Labs participates in affiliate marketing programs. This means that when you click certain links on our Site and make a purchase, we may receive a commission from the product manufacturer or their affiliate platform. The price you pay is the same whether you purchase through our link or navigate directly to the product website.

Our Affiliate Partners

We currently work with affiliate programs on the following platforms:

  • ClickBank — digital products and supplements
  • BuyGoods — health and wellness supplements
  • Direct brand affiliate programs — individual supplement brands

How Affiliate Relationships Affect Our Content

They don't.

Our editorial analysis is conducted the same way regardless of whether we have an affiliate relationship with a product. We analyze ingredients against published research, investigate consumer complaints honestly, and assess purchasing safety — for every product we cover.

Having an affiliate link on a page does not mean we recommend the product unconditionally. It means we have covered the product editorially and provided a purchasing option for readers who decide the product is appropriate for them after reading our analysis.

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines on endorsements and testimonials, Health Reviews Labs discloses affiliate relationships on all pages containing affiliate links. This disclosure appears in the footer of relevant pages and in this dedicated disclosure page.

Editorial Independence

Health Reviews Labs does not accept payment for positive coverage, guaranteed favorable reviews, or editorial placement. Products are selected for coverage based on consumer search interest, ingredient novelty, and category relevance — not commercial arrangements.
