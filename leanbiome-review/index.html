What Is LeanBiome?

LeanBiome is a weight management supplement built on a specific research finding from Harvard and Stanford: gut microbiome composition is a stronger predictor of weight management success than caloric intake alone. It combines 9 clinically studied "lean bacteria" strains with Greenselect Phytosome, a caffeine-free, high-absorption green tea extract.

The Lean Bacteria Mechanism

Specific bacterial strains produce short-chain fatty acids — butyrate, propionate, acetate — during fiber fermentation. These compounds regulate fat-storage hormones including leptin, reduce inflammatory signaling that promotes fat storage, improve insulin sensitivity, and modulate appetite through gut-derived hunger hormones. People with higher populations of these specific strains tend to maintain healthy weight more easily, even at similar caloric intake to people with depleted populations — which explains the frustrating experience of eating similarly to others while struggling more with weight.

LeanBiome Ingredients

9 Lean Bacteria Strains

Including Lactobacillus Gasseri, Rhamnosus, and Fermentum — each selected because published clinical research linked that specific strain to body composition outcomes, not general gut health.

Greenselect Phytosome

A caffeine-free green tea extract bound to phosphatidylcholine, dramatically improving absorption over standard green tea extracts. Multiple clinical trials show effects on body weight and metabolic rate.

DRcaps Delayed-Release Technology

Ensures probiotic strains survive stomach acid and reach the small intestine alive, where standard capsules expose bacteria to pH levels that kill a significant percentage before they arrive.
