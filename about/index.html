Our Mission

The supplement industry is worth over $150 billion globally — and a significant portion of that value is built on confusion, exaggerated claims, and consumers who don't have the time or tools to evaluate what they're buying.

Health Reviews Labs exists to close that gap. Every review we publish is based on three questions: What does the research actually say about these ingredients? What are real users reporting about this product? And where can you buy it safely if you decide to try it?

We don't accept payment from manufacturers to influence our reviews. We don't hide our affiliate relationships. And we don't pretend that supplements are a substitute for medical care — but we do believe that informed consumers deserve access to honest, well-researched analysis.

How We Review Products

1

Ingredient Research

We cross-reference each active ingredient against published nutritional and clinical research to evaluate the evidence behind manufacturer claims.

2

Formula Analysis

We evaluate dosages, ingredient quality, manufacturing standards, and whether the formula is transparent about what's actually inside each serving.

3

User Feedback

We analyze verified customer reviews, complaints, and user-reported experiences from multiple sources to identify consistent patterns — positive and negative.

4

Honest Verdict

We publish a balanced conclusion that includes both the strengths and limitations of each product — including who it's best suited for and who should avoid it.
