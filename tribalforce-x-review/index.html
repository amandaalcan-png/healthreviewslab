All 8 Ingredients

St. John's Wort

Contains hypericin and hyperforin that modulate serotonin, dopamine, and norepinephrine reuptake — reducing the performance anxiety response and supporting the relaxed-confident psychological state where arousal signals transmit most effectively.

Phosphatidylserine 4% Complex

The phospholipid component of neuronal cell membranes — essential for neurotransmitter receptor integrity and cortisol regulation. Clinical evidence for reducing the HPA axis stress response that otherwise suppresses testosterone and performance.

Bacopa Monnieri Extract

Reduces anxiety through GABA system modulation in the hippocampus while improving synaptic plasticity and signal quality in regions involved in memory and executive function. The combination of anxiety reduction and cognitive enhancement is unique among botanicals.

Ginkgo Biloba

Flavonoids and terpenoids (ginkgolides) improve platelet function and vascular elasticity — increasing blood flow to both brain tissue and peripheral vascular beds. Documented vasodilatory effects with particular relevance to microcirculation in erectile tissue.

Vinpocetine

Derived from the Vinca minor plant — selectively increases cerebral blood flow and oxygen utilization. Improves the quality of neural signaling during the arousal pathway by ensuring adequate oxygenation of the neurons involved in the arousal cascade.

N-Acetyl L-Carnitine HCl (NALC)

Crosses the blood-brain barrier and provides mitochondrial energy substrate to neurons — supporting sustained neural activity and the cellular energy required for consistent performance over the course of an encounter.

Huperzine-A

Acetylcholinesterase inhibitor — prevents the enzymatic breakdown of acetylcholine, maintaining higher levels of the neurotransmitter involved in focus, attention, and the neural-muscular signaling relevant to sexual response.

L-Glutamine

The most abundant amino acid in the bloodstream — supports protein synthesis and has been studied for its ability to stimulate growth hormone release, providing the hormonal substrate that supports testosterone production in the Leydig cell pathway.

Drug interaction warning: St. John's Wort has significant interactions with multiple medications including antidepressants (SSRI serotonin syndrome risk), blood thinners, birth control, and HIV medications. Huperzine-A requires physician review for anyone on cholinergic or anticholinergic medications. Consult your physician before use if taking any prescription medication.
