What Is Prime Biome?

Prime Biome is a gut health supplement built around the gut-skin axis — the increasingly well-documented connection between gut microbiome composition and skin health, mediated significantly through immune tolerance. It combines four specific probiotic strains with prebiotic fiber to support both digestive comfort and the immune calibration that affects skin reactivity.

The Immune Tolerance Mechanism

Immune tolerance is the immune system's calibrated ability to distinguish harmless triggers — food particles, environmental compounds, the body's own tissue — from genuine threats requiring an inflammatory response. A significant portion of this calibration happens in the gut, where roughly 70% of immune tissue resides. When gut microbiome composition is imbalanced, this calibration can drift, contributing to inappropriate inflammatory responses that frequently manifest as skin reactivity, since skin is one of the body's most visible inflammatory readouts.

Prime Biome Ingredients

Bacillus Coagulans

A spore-forming probiotic that survives stomach acid reliably, supporting consistent colonization and immune signaling.

Lactobacillus Plantarum

Documented research on tight junction integrity in the gut lining, reducing the "leaky gut" component that can trigger systemic inflammatory responses.

Bifidobacterium Longum

Associated with reduced inflammatory cytokine production and improved gut barrier function.

Saccharomyces Boulardii

A beneficial yeast (not bacteria) with documented research on immune modulation and resilience during antibiotic use.

Inulin & FOS (Prebiotic Fiber)

Feeds the beneficial strains directly, supporting their establishment and ongoing colonization in the gut.

Note: Take probiotic supplements at least 2 hours apart from antibiotic doses, since antibiotics can kill beneficial bacteria along with harmful ones if taken simultaneously.
