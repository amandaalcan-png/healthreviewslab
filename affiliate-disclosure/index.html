Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Health Reviews Lab is reader-supported. When you purchase through links on our site, we may earn an affiliate commission — at no extra cost to you. This never influences the honesty of our reviews.

What Is an Affiliate Link?

Some links on this website are affiliate links. This means that if you click on the link and purchase the item, we will receive a commission from the sale. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the seller's website.

Affiliate commissions help fund the research and writing that goes into every review on this site. Without this income, we could not continue to provide free, independent analysis to our readers.

Our Affiliate Partners

We participate in affiliate programs operated by the following platforms and networks:

  • ClickBank — digital health and wellness products
  • Digistore24 — health supplements and wellness programs
  • Other product-specific affiliate programs as disclosed in individual reviews

Our Editorial Independence

We want to be absolutely clear: our affiliate relationships do not influence our reviews or recommendations. The following principles govern how we operate:

  • We research products independently before deciding whether to review or recommend them
  • We do not accept payment from manufacturers to write positive reviews
  • We do not receive free products in exchange for favorable coverage
  • Our ratings and verdicts are based on ingredient research, formula analysis, and user feedback — not on commission rates
  • We will recommend against a product even if it carries a high commission, if the evidence doesn't support it

FTC Compliance

In accordance with the Federal Trade Commission's guidelines (16 CFR Part 255), we disclose our affiliate relationships clearly on this page and within individual reviews where affiliate links appear. We believe in full transparency with our readers.

If you have any questions about our affiliate relationships or how they affect our content, please contact us.

Your Purchase Decision

Our reviews are intended to inform — not to replace professional medical advice.
