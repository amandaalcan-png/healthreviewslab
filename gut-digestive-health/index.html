Why Most Digestive Supplements Underdeliver

Chronic digestive dysfunction — bloating, constipation, irregular bowel movements, post-meal heaviness — typically involves four interacting problems: sluggish gut motility, insufficient digestive enzymes, microbiome imbalance, and intestinal inflammation. Single-ingredient supplements address one of these. The symptoms driven by the other three mechanisms remain active. Health Reviews Labs reviews digestive supplements based on how many of these mechanisms they address and with what quality of evidence — not on marketing claims.
