Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

When you visit our Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device and browsing behavior, including:

We do not operate comment sections, subscription forms, or user accounts on this Site. We do not actively collect personally identifiable information from visitors.

Cookies

Our Site uses cookies — small text files stored on your device — for the following purposes:

You can disable cookies in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of third-party platforms we link to.

Affiliate partner platforms — including ClickBank, BuyGoods, and others — have their own privacy policies governing data they collect when you visit their sites.

We use the following third-party services that may collect data according to their own privacy policies:

We do not store personally identifiable information. Analytics data is retained according to Google Analytics default settings (26 months) and is used only in aggregate form.
