Key Mechanisms — What the Research Covers

Health Reviews Labs analyzes supplement formulas against publicly available clinical research. The following mechanism summaries reflect the published literature on ingredients commonly found in cognitive support formulas — not claims about specific products.

Nerve Growth Factor & Neuroplasticity

Lion's Mane Mushroom is studied for stimulating nerve growth factor (NGF) production, a protein essential for neuron growth and maintenance. A 2009 Phytotherapy Research study found measurable cognitive improvements in older adults over 16 weeks, with effects diminishing after supplementation stopped — suggesting an active, ongoing mechanism.

Memory Consolidation

Bacopa Monnieri has substantial clinical research specifically on memory consolidation, the process converting short-term memories into stable long-term storage. Multiple controlled trials show improved recall scores after 8-12 weeks of consistent use.

Cerebral Blood Flow

Ginkgo Biloba's circulatory effects support oxygen and nutrient delivery to neural tissue, addressing the vascular component of age-related cognitive decline that compounds alongside cellular-level changes.
