What Is CitrusBurn?

CitrusBurn is a caffeine-free thermogenic fat-burning supplement built around p-synephrine, a compound derived from bitter orange that selectively activates beta-3 adrenergic receptors — distinct from the beta-1 and beta-2 receptors most stimulant-based fat burners rely on.

The Beta-3 Receptor Mechanism

Beta-3 adrenergic receptors are concentrated in adipose (fat) tissue and, when activated, trigger lipolysis — the breakdown of stored fat for energy — and increase thermogenesis, the metabolic heat production process that burns calories. Because beta-3 receptors follow a distinct regulatory pathway from beta-1/beta-2 receptors, they remain comparatively more responsive in people whose years of caffeine or stimulant use have desensitized the receptors typical fat burners target.

CitrusBurn Ingredients

P-Synephrine

Selectively activates beta-3 adrenergic receptors, triggering lipolysis and thermogenesis through a different pathway than caffeine.

Coleus Forskohlii

Forskolin activates adenylate cyclase, increasing cAMP — a second messenger that amplifies the lipolytic signal from beta-3 activation.

White Willow Bark

Contains salicin, which has historically been paired with synephrine-type compounds to potentiate thermogenic effects.

Green Tea EGCG

Inhibits the enzyme that breaks down norepinephrine, extending the duration of the thermogenic signal.

Note: Anyone with cardiovascular conditions or high blood pressure should consult their physician before using p-synephrine-containing products.
