Editorial Policy

Health Reviews Labs does not accept payment for positive coverage, sponsored reviews, or guaranteed favorable assessments. Our editorial position is independent of commercial relationships.

If you represent a supplement brand and would like your product considered for editorial analysis, we evaluate all products by the same methodology — ingredient quality, clinical evidence, manufacturing standards, and consumer feedback. No payment is accepted or required for coverage consideration.

Affiliate partnerships are separate from editorial coverage. Being an affiliate partner does not influence our analysis or guarantee positive coverage. See our Affiliate Disclosure for full details.
