What Is iGenics?

iGenics is a premium eye health supplement made by ScienceGenics — formulated with 12 natural, vegan ingredients in an FDA-registered, GMP-certified US facility. It contains zero fillers, zero artificial additives, and uses clinically relevant dosages based on the AREDS-2 research protocol established by the National Eye Institute.

The formula targets three root causes of vision decline: oxidative stress damaging retinal cells, inflammation in ocular tissue, and insufficient blood flow to the eyes. Each of the 12 ingredients addresses at least one of these mechanisms with published research support.

The AREDS-2 Research Foundation

The Age-Related Eye Disease Study 2 (AREDS-2) is one of the most significant clinical studies ever conducted on nutritional eye health. Funded by the National Eye Institute and spanning 20+ years, it established that a specific combination of nutrients — Lutein, Zeaxanthin, Vitamin C, Vitamin E, Zinc, and Copper — reduces the risk of progressing to advanced age-related macular degeneration (AMD) by 25% over 5 years.

This is not marketing language. These are results from a randomized, double-blind, placebo-controlled clinical trial on 4,000+ participants, published in the journal Ophthalmology and cited by every major ophthalmological association. iGenics is one of the few consumer supplements formulated to exactly match these clinically validated nutrient combinations — and then goes further with additional ingredients not in the original AREDS-2 protocol.

iGenics Ingredients — 12 Clinically Selected Nutrients

Lutein 20mg — AREDS-2 Core + Double Dose

A carotenoid that concentrates in the macula and acts as a natural blue light filter and antioxidant shield. The AREDS-2 protocol uses 10mg; iGenics uses 20mg — double the studied dose. A 2019 Ophthalmology study confirmed regular Lutein intake improves macular health, enhances night vision, and significantly reduces AMD risk.

Zeaxanthin 2mg — AREDS-2 Core

Works synergistically with Lutein in the macula to protect eye cells from harmful light exposure and improve visual clarity. The AREDS-2 dose is 2mg — iGenics matches this exactly.

Bilberry Extract 480mg — Vision & Night Sight

Rich in anthocyanosides that strengthen capillary walls supplying the retina, improve blood flow to ocular tissue, and reduce eye fatigue. The 480mg dose is substantially higher than most competing supplements.

Saffron — Retinal Protection

Saffron's active compound (crocin) improves light sensitivity in photoreceptors and protects against light-induced retinal damage. Research in Investigative Ophthalmology & Visual Science confirmed significant improvements in visual function in AMD patients.

Ginkgo Biloba — Ocular Blood Flow

Improves circulation to the capillaries supplying the retina and optic nerve, supporting visual clarity particularly in low-light conditions and after long screen sessions.

Turmeric 350mg — Anti-Inflammatory

Curcumin provides anti-inflammatory protection for eye tissue. Chronic ocular inflammation is now recognized as a major driver of age-related eye conditions including AMD and glaucoma.

Note: iGenics is a dietary supplement, not a treatment for diagnosed eye conditions. It does not replace glasses, contacts, or medical treatment. Consult your ophthalmologist before starting if you have a diagnosed eye condition.
