Emerging research from multiple institutions has identified Saffron as one of the most potent natural compounds for retinal health. Ginkgo Biloba — Ocular Blood Flow

Improves blood circulation throughout the body including the delicate capillaries supplying the retina and optic nerve. Better blood flow means better oxygen and nutrient delivery to ocular tissue — directly supporting visual clarity, particularly in low-light conditions and after long screen sessions. Ginkgo's neuroprotective properties also support the optic nerve pathways involved in visual signal transmission.

Turmeric 350mg — Anti-Inflammatory

Curcumin in Turmeric provides powerful anti-inflammatory protection for eye tissue. Chronic low-grade ocular inflammation is now recognized as a major driver of age-related eye conditions including AMD and glaucoma. At 350mg — a therapeutically relevant dose — Turmeric helps calm this inflammation at the cellular level, protecting the delicate structures of the eye from long-term inflammatory damage.

Vitamins C, E, A + Zinc + Copper + Black Pepper

Vitamins C and E are the core antioxidant vitamins from the AREDS-2 protocol. Vitamin A is essential for the production of rhodopsin — the light-sensitive pigment in rod cells that enables low-light and night vision.
